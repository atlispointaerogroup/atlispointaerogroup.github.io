Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

When you use the contact form or pilot application form on this Site, the information you enter — including your name, email address, and message — is used to compose an email sent directly from your own email application to AtlisPoint. We do not operate a server that receives or stores this information independently of your email provider.

Information Collected Automatically

We do not currently use analytics software, tracking pixels, or advertising cookies on this Site. Standard web server logs may record basic technical information such as your IP address, browser type, and pages visited. This information is not linked to any personally identifiable information and is used solely for maintaining the security and operation of the Site.

Cookies

This Site does not currently use cookies for tracking or advertising purposes. Basic session cookies may be set by your browser as part of standard web functionality. You may disable cookies through your browser settings without affecting your ability to use this Site.

How We Use Your Information

Any information you voluntarily submit through our contact or application forms is used solely to respond to your inquiry or evaluate your application. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

We may use your information to:

— Respond to inquiries submitted through the Site

— Evaluate pilot or professional applications

— Communicate with you about services you have requested

Because form submissions on this Site are processed through your own email client, AtlisPoint retains only the emails received in our inbox. We retain correspondence for as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was sent or as required by applicable law. International Visitors and GDPR

If you are located in the European Union, United Kingdom, or European Economic Area, you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and applicable UK law:

— Right of Access: You may request a copy of any personal data we hold about you.

— Right to Rectification: You may request correction of inaccurate personal data.

— Right to Erasure: You may request deletion of your personal data where there is no lawful basis for continued processing.

— Right to Restrict Processing: You may request that we limit how we use your data.

— Right to Data Portability: You may request a copy of your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

— Right to Object: You may object to processing of your personal data in certain circumstances.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@atlispoint.com. We will respond within 30 days.

The legal basis for processing any personal data you submit is your consent, given by voluntarily submitting a form or sending us an email.
